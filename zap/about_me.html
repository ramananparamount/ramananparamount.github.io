Who We Are

Business Systems Education & Research Institute (BSERI) is an Education venture, engaged in the Management System Standards knowledge industry, focussing on MSS implementation needs of the industry.

BSERI is the result of in-depth thinking by the stakeholders of Paramount Dataware, that identified the gaps in the MSS training industry and enabled the organization to visualize opportunities that can contribute to a significant positive transformation in MSS Training content, method, format and approach.

Vision

Become one among the top 3 training ventures, in India, providing ISO Management Systems Standards training by 2020

Mission

Act as a Change agent for infusing real value in ISO MSS Standards Training by Adapting web & mobile technology options Building the courses, interactive by design Paper-free classroom, with intelligent online alternatives

Our Perceptions

BSERI is the outcome of purposeful study of MSS training courses, conducted by various institutions. Most of these are invariably ISO MSS Lead Auditor Courses.

BSERI’s solid understanding is - professionals, who work in organizations implementing ISO MSS, require Strong Implementation Skills for effective and objective implementation and post-implementation maintenance of business systems.BSERI envisions evolving itself as a perpetual game changer in the Management Systems training industry by customizing courses and their delivery to closely meet industry needs and aspirations.

Initiatives underway include blended training delivery using E-learning and M-learning besides pure classroom delivery.

“Making the learning not just useful, but also enjoyable, is one of the creed of BSERI”

Organizations require skill and competence to manage their business systems. This business imperative drives BSERI’s training courses that are packed with content and delivered powerfully, igniting the imagination of participants and enhance their asset value to their respective organizations.

The Institute wants to be known for its serious and sincere interest to build future through competence.

Engaging with us takes you a step ahead in your career

Think wide & wild

Our USP

BSERI's training strategies have been developed on our strong understanding of the blended relationship of organizational business processes and ISO Management System Standard requirements. This will be a key game-changer.

It is the Lead Auditor Courses that are the mainstay in the ISO MSS Training industry today. BSERI aims to create a market for ISO MSS Implementation Courses, besides a larger market for ISO Management Systems Foundation (MSF) courses and other programs that will address the gaps in the ISO Standards implementation & maintenance across industries.

“BSERI'S head advantage is its focus on capability-build through competence development. We truly believe in FUTURE THROUGH COMPETENCE”

BSERI’s trainers have reliable and practical ISO MSS implementation consulting experience, besides having proven experience in delivering training courses. Some of them have good experience in Auditing as well.

The Institute shall endeavour building appropriate & comprehensive competence through training courses and other knowledge-related programs, keeping the focus on BSERI’s tagline, “Future through Competence”.

BSERI’s certificates issued to the Course-participants shall receive respect and recognition across industries. The Institute shall take every step to be seen by the industry as one of the Most Effective Training Institutions that aim to raise the competence of professionals and thus the competitive strengths of business organizations that employ them.
