BSERI Course Structure

Our commitment towards your Future Through Competence

Getting Started - Foundation Course

A new way of education. There is no learning here, this is pure unlearning. Journey towards your future. On the mark, Get Set, Go into the Foundation course.

Break the Jinx - Professional Course

After a strong foundation, it is time to jump into the real work. Real work in the form of hands on exercises, case studies and tests that would mould a professional into an hyper-professional. Keep running

Reach the Zenith - Expert Course

Learning is a continuity. Professionals aspire to go beyond just being a professional. Journey towards the peak is not far away though it is not a difficult for an hyper professional. Calm sea cannot make skillful sailors.
